Hi there, it’s Cindy Zhu. I am an HCI graduate student at the University of Michigan, where I focus on user experience design. I have shipped features to million users by spending two summers interning at Tencent (this summer on the Tencent Music app JOOX, and last summer on the Tencent PUBG mobile game).

I majored in Environmental Economics and Policy when I was an undergrad at UC Berkeley. At the same time, I took a lot of business classes and design thinking classes. This interdisciplinary background helps me understand an organization’s business goals and key metrics before I start to design.
I enjoy everything pop-culture related, comedy, and observing people at airports, grocery stores… wherever I go. Often I find myself coming back to design more anyways. While I am traveling and observing, I am also shooting streets.
